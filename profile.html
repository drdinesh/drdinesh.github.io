Profile

I am an enthusiastic, self-motivated, reliable, responsible and hard working person having more than 18 years of teaching and research experience, worked at various academic positions, a mature team worker and adaptable to all challenging situations. I am able to work well both in a team environment as well as using own initiative. I am able to work well under pressure and adhere to strict deadlines. I believe in three mantras of teaching. These are: continuous hard work, practical approach and making students learn the concepts and apply them rather than cramming.

I trust that getting maximum output from a learning process is only possible if both students and teachers work in close liaison and also work hard during the course. I desire the students must put in some extra efforts in attempting the academic assignments, practical problems to have more grip on the subject. I feel it becomes more challenging for the teachers if students come prepared in the class. I have to work hard which conclusively helps me to enhance my subject knowledge.

I always welcome queries from students related to the subject or any personal problems. I believe that my mission as a teacher is to lead students through the difficult areas in the subject. I believe it is impossible to get success without putting sincere efforts, hard work and learning from failures.

As a testament to my teaching ability, I received Best Engineering College Teacher Award (Punjab State) from Indian Society of Technical Education (ISTE) in the year 2017.
